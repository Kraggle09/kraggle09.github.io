# Redstone Weekly - A Minecraft Podcast

## About the show
Redstone weekly was (as the title may suggest) a Minecraft podcast. I covered the latest news, play Minecraft, and generally just talk about Minecraft.

## Brief history
I created the show in November 2019, and uploaded the first episode January 2020. I uploaded semi-frequently (averaging 1-2 episodes per month), before abandoning it in Spring 2025.

## Some quick specs
**Schedule:** On hiatus  
**Seasons:** 3  
**Episodes:** 114  
**Years Active:** November 2019-May 2025
